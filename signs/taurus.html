Taurus

April 20 – May 20 • Earth Sign

Personality

Taurus has a unique energy connected to the Earth element. This sign grows stronger through self-awareness and positive choices.

Love Life

In love, Taurus needs honesty, respect, and emotional understanding. A healthy relationship helps this sign feel confident and safe.

Success

Taurus succeeds by using natural strengths with patience, discipline, and clear goals.

Mind

The Taurus mind works best with peace, purpose, and balance. Self-control helps avoid stress.

Strengths

  • Patient
  • Loyal
  • Practical

Advice

Trust yourself, keep learning, and do not compare your journey with others.
