Gemini

May 21 – June 20 • Air Sign

Personality

Gemini has a unique energy connected to the Air element. This sign grows stronger through self-awareness and positive choices.

Love Life

In love, Gemini needs honesty, respect, and emotional understanding. A healthy relationship helps this sign feel confident and safe.

Success

Gemini succeeds by using natural strengths with patience, discipline, and clear goals.

Mind

The Gemini mind works best with peace, purpose, and balance. Self-control helps avoid stress.

Strengths

  • Curious
  • Social
  • Quick-minded

Advice

Trust yourself, keep learning, and do not compare your journey with others.
