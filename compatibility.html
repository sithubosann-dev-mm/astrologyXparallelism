Best Element Matches

Fire + Air

Fire brings passion. Air brings ideas. Together, they create excitement and movement.

Earth + Water

Earth gives stability. Water gives emotion. Together, they create trust and comfort.

Fire + Fire

Strong chemistry, but both people need patience and respect.

Air + Air

Great communication, but they should avoid overthinking everything.

Earth + Earth

Stable and loyal, but they should keep the relationship fresh.

Water + Water

Deep emotional connection, but both need healthy boundaries.

Compatibility Advice
