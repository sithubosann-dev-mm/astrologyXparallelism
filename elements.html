Fire, Earth, Air & Water

Each zodiac sign belongs to an element that describes its natural energy.

Fire Signs

Aries, Leo, Sagittarius

Passionate, brave, creative, energetic, and action-focused.

Earth Signs

Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn

Stable, practical, patient, hardworking, and grounded.

Air Signs

Gemini, Libra, Aquarius

Social, intellectual, communicative, curious, and idea-focused.

Water Signs

Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces

Emotional, intuitive, caring, deep, and imaginative.
