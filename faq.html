FAQ

Common questions about zodiac signs and astrology.

What is a zodiac sign?

A zodiac sign is based on your birth date and is used in astrology to describe personality and life energy.

Are zodiac signs always accurate?

Astrology is symbolic and fun. It can guide self-reflection, but it should not control your choices.

What are the four elements?

The four elements are Fire, Earth, Air, and Water. Each element gives signs a different style of energy.

Can I add real daily horoscope updates?
